Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round, leavened dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients, then baked at a high temperature

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead
  3. Rise
  4. Punch & shape
  5. Top it
  6. Bake
